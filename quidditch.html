👥 Quidditch Positions 👥

⚡

Seeker

Chases the Golden Snitch! The most important position! ⚡

🎯 Objective: Catch the Snitch
🏆 Points: 150 Points
🌟 Famous: Harry Potter
🎯

Chaser

Scores goals with the Quaffle! Team's main scorers! 🎯

🎯 Objective: Score Goals
🏆 Points: 10 Points
🌟 Famous: Angelina Johnson
🛡️

Keeper

Defends the goal posts! Last line of defense! 🛡️

🎯 Objective: Block Goals
🏆 Points: 0 Points
🌟 Famous: Oliver Wood
🏏

Beater

Protects team from Bludgers! Uses bats! 🏏

🎯 Objective: Defend Team
🏆 Points: 0 Points
🌟 Famous: Fred & George
