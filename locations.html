🏰 Hogwarts School of Witchcraft & Wizardry 🏰

The most magical school in the world! 🎓

🏰

Great Hall

The heart of Hogwarts, where students gather for meals and special events. The enchanted ceiling shows the sky outside!

Location: Ground Floor
Special Features: Enchanted Ceiling, House Tables
Notable Events: Sorting Ceremony, Feasts
📚

Library

Home to thousands of magical books and the Restricted Section. Madame Pince guards it fiercely!

Location: First Floor
Special Features: Restricted Section, Study Areas
Librarian: Madame Pince
🦁

Gryffindor Tower

The cozy common room for Gryffindor students, accessed through a portrait of the Fat Lady.

Location: Seventh Floor
Guardian: The Fat Lady
Password: Changes Regularly
🏛️

Dungeons

Home to Slytherin House and the Potions classroom. Cold, dark, and mysterious!

Location: Below Ground
Special Features: Slytherin Common Room, Potions Lab
Professor: Severus Snape

🏘️ Diagon Alley 🏘️

The magical shopping district! 🛍️

🏦

Gringotts Bank

The wizarding bank run by goblins. Home to the Potter family vault and countless other magical treasures!

Owners: Goblins
Special Features: Underground Vaults, Dragon Guards
Notable Vault: Vault 713 (Philosopher's Stone)
🪄

Ollivanders

The finest wand shop in the world. "The wand chooses the wizard, Mr. Potter."

Owner: Garrick Ollivander
Special Features: Wand Fitting, Wand History
Famous Wand: Harry's Holly & Phoenix Feather
📚

Flourish & Blotts

The premier bookstore for all magical literature and textbooks.

Specialty: Magical Books & Textbooks
Notable Books: Monster Book of Monsters
Events: Book Signings
🧪

Apothecary

The magical pharmacy and potion ingredients shop. Smells of herbs and magical creatures!

Specialty: Potion Ingredients
Notable Items: Dragon Liver, Unicorn Horn
Smell: Herbs & Creatures

🌍 Other Magical Locations 🌍

Beyond Hogwarts and Diagon Alley! 🗺️

🏠

The Burrow

The Weasley family home - a magical, ramshackle house that defies gravity!

Location: Ottery St. Catchpole
Special Features: Gravity-Defying Architecture
Residents: The Weasley Family
🏛️

Ministry of Magic

The government headquarters of the wizarding world, hidden beneath London.

Location: Underground London
Access: Phone Booth, Floo Network
Departments: Auror Office, Magical Law
🌲

Forbidden Forest

The mysterious forest bordering Hogwarts, home to dangerous creatures and secrets.

Location: Hogwarts Grounds
Creatures: Centaur, Acromantula, Unicorn
Danger Level: Extremely High
🏔️

Hogsmeade Village

The only all-wizarding village in Britain, a popular weekend destination for Hogwarts students.

Location: Near Hogwarts
Notable Shops: Honeydukes, Three Broomsticks
Access: Hogsmeade Weekends
