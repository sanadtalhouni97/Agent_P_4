⭐ Most Popular Spells ⭐

💡

Lumos

Charm

Creates light at the tip of your wand! Perfect for exploring dark places! 🌟

🎯 Effect: Creates light
📚 Difficulty: Beginner
👨‍🏫 Taught by: Professor Flitwick
🗣️ Incantation: "Lumos"
🪶

Wingardium Leviosa

Charm

Makes objects levitate! Remember: "It's LeviOsa, not LeviosA!" 🪶

🎯 Effect: Levitation
📚 Difficulty: Intermediate
👨‍🏫 Taught by: Professor Flitwick
🗣️ Incantation: "Wingardium Leviosa"
🦌

Expecto Patronum

Charm

Conjures a Patronus to drive away Dementors! Think of your happiest memory! 🦌

🎯 Effect: Patronus Charm
📚 Difficulty: Advanced
👨‍🏫 Taught by: Professor Lupin
🗣️ Incantation: "Expecto Patronum"
🔓

Alohomora

Charm

Unlocks doors and other locked objects! The thief's friend! 🔓

🎯 Effect: Unlocking
📚 Difficulty: Beginner
👨‍🏫 Taught by: Professor Flitwick
🗣️ Incantation: "Alohomora"
🛡️

Protego

Charm

Creates a magical shield to deflect spells and protect you! 🛡️

🎯 Effect: Shield Charm
📚 Difficulty: Intermediate
👨‍🏫 Taught by: Professor Flitwick
🗣️ Incantation: "Protego"
🔥

Incendio

Charm

Creates fire from your wand! Perfect for lighting fires or defense! 🔥

🎯 Effect: Fire Creation
📚 Difficulty: Intermediate
👨‍🏫 Taught by: Professor McGonagall
🗣️ Incantation: "Incendio"
