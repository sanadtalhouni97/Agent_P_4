About Hogwarts

Founded over a thousand years ago by the four greatest witches and wizards of the age, Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry stands as a beacon of magical education and discovery.

Located in the Scottish Highlands, this ancient castle has been the training ground for countless generations of witches and wizards, preparing them for a life of magic, adventure, and wonder.
